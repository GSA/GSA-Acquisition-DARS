SUBPART 5849.70 – SPECIAL TERMINATION REQUIREMENTS

Parent topic: PART 5849 - TERMINATION OF CONTRACTS

5849.7001 Congressional Notification on Significant Contract Terminations

The contracting officer shall obtain coordination from legal counsel, the CoCO, the HCO, the OSBP (if the action involves a small business), and approval from the HCA prior to the release of any termination notice under this section.
