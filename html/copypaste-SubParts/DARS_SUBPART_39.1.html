Subpart 39.1 - GENERAL

Parent topic: Part 39 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

39.101 Policy.

(S-90) Unless there is an associated service being offered with a software subscription or softwaremaintenance agreement, and that associated service is billed separately, the acquisition of software subscriptions or software maintenance agreements will be acquired as a product. Further, IT Value Added Reseller (ITVAR) excepted acquisitions with the NAICS code 541519 will be acquired as a product.
