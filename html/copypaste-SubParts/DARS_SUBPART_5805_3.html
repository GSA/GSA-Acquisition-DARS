SUBPART 5805.3 – SYNOPSIS OF CONTRACT AWARDS

Parent topic: PART 5805 - PUBLICIZING CONTRACT ACTIONS

5805.303 Announcement of Contract Awards

(a)(i) For contract actions awarded by a DISA contracting officer, regardless of the mission partner, the contracting officer shall prepare a Public Announcement when the face value of a contractual action exceeds $7.5M.

(a)(i)(C) Orders do not require a Public Announcement.

(S-90) Required format for a “Public Announcement” is in the DARS PGI 5805.303(a)(i)(S-90).

(S-91) Procedures for preparation and routing the Public Announcement is in the DARS PGI 5805.303(a)(i)(S-91).
