SUBPART 5812.1 – ACQUISTION OF COMMERCIAL PRODUCTS AND COMMERCIAL SERVICES

Parent topic: PART 5812 - ACQUISITION OF COMMERCIAL PRODUCTS AND COMMERCIAL SERVICES

5812.102 Applicability

(a)(ii)(B)(S-90) The HCA may use the authority outlined in DFARS 212.102 to deviate from Commercial Determinations, with or without overturning the entire Commercial Determination, if it is not in the best interest of the DISA to pursue a commercial acquisition.
