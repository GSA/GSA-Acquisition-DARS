5801.670 Appointment of Property Administrators and Plant Clearance Officers

(a) For all DISA funded requirements, the Agency authority to appoint or terminate (in writing) property administrators (PA) and plant clearance officers (PLCO), and to designate PAs and PLCOs is delegated to the J4, Facilities and Logistics. For Non-DISA funded requirements, the authority to appoint or terminate (in writing) PAs and PLCOs is delegated to the Contracting Officer.
